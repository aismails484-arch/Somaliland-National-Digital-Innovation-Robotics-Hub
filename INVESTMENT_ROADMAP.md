# Strategic Investment Roadmap

## 1. Funding Allocation
* **Infrastructure (40%):** Establishing regional tech hubs and renewable energy grids.
* **Research & Development (30%):** Designing Somaliland-specific AI and Robotics solutions.
* **Human Capital (20%):** Recruiting and certifying the specialized "Carbisan" workforce.
* **Operations & Oversight (10%):** Ensuring continuous drone and robotic auditing for transparency.

## 2. Socio-Economic ROI (Return on Investment)
The SPH Global framework focuses on sustainable impact:
* **Job Creation:** Empowering local engineers and international also the researchers.
* **Cost Efficiency:** Reducing industrial energy costs by 80-90%.
* **Digital Infrastructure:** Providing the foundation for international trade and data sovereignty.
